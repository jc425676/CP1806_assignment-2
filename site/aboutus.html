About Us

Mission

FRIAS is an ecological Kids Wear company that aims to provide the highest quality, and most comfortable clothing for kids of all ages as well as tailor services with accordance to the latest fashion trend.

Our Core Values

Creativity, passion and to operate under a social and ecological protocol base on moderation, intelligence and cooperation.
